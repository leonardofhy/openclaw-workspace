# Cycle c-20260310-1315 | learn | Q040 groundwork

**Task**: Q040 — Audio-RAVEL × ESN leakage causal scorecard
**Paper**: Zhao et al. 2601.03115 — "Discovering and Causally Validating ESNs in LALMs"

## Problem
Do LALMs contain emotion-sensitive neurons (ESNs) that are *causally* responsible for emotion recognition? 
Q040's goal: compute RAVEL Cause/Isolate scores under 2x2 emotion-speaker controls to characterize "leakage."

## Method (Zhao et al.)
- Models: Qwen2.5-Omni-7B, Kimi-Audio, Audio Flamingo 3
- Benchmarks: IEMOCAP, MELD, MSP-Podcast
- Hook SwiGLU gate activations in decoder MLPs → collect on correct SER items
- 4 selectors: LAP (frequency), LAPE (entropy), MAD (magnitude contrast), Contrast
- Intervention: ablation (→ degrade) + amplification (→ steer)

## Key Results
1. **Self-cross structure confirmed**: ablating ESN_e degrades emotion e >> other emotions → causal specificity
2. **Magnitude/contrast selectors >> LAP/LAPE**: frequency-only selection insufficient for isolation
3. **Layer clustering**: ESNs at layers 0, 6-8, 19-22 (not uniform). Early = acoustic pickup, late = decision
4. **Partial cross-dataset transfer**: emotion-specific, asymmetric
5. **Steering works**: amplify ESN_e → biases predictions toward e; label-free injection (2-Pass, Mix, Union) more variable

## Connection to Q040 (RAVEL Cause/Isolate framing)
- RAVEL Cause(e) ≈ accuracy drop when ablating ESN_e on target emotion e samples → Zhao does this
- RAVEL Isolate(e) ≈ 1 - avg accuracy drop on *non-target* emotions → **not explicitly computed in paper**
- **Leakage hypothesis**: ESNs for emotion e also activate for speaker identity → Isolate_speaker < Isolate_emotion
- Q040 adds 2x2 control: {same vs. different speaker} × {target vs. non-target emotion}
  - If ablating ESN_happy kills "angry-female-speaker" more than "angry-male-speaker" → speaker leaked into happy ESN

## Scorecard Design for Q040
```
2x2 grid per emotion e:
              | Target emotion | Non-target emotion |
Same speaker  |   Cause_ss     |   Leakage_ss       |
Diff speaker  |   Cause_ds     |   Leakage_ds       |

Cause = mean accuracy drop on target
Leakage = mean accuracy drop on non-target (ideally ~0)
Speaker leakage = Leakage_ss > Leakage_ds (speaker identity encoded in ESN)
```
- Magnitude contrast selector is the right baseline (Zhao's best)

## Open Questions
1. Does speaker identity systematically bleed into emotion ESNs (the key Q040 hypothesis)?
2. Which layer range shows highest leakage? (early layers likely most speaker-confounded)
3. Does contrast selector already partially disentangle speaker from emotion?
