## Key Design Choice

Accented speaker beams are modeled with anti-correlation between log_prob and AND-frac:
- Beam 0: LM-biased guess → high log_prob, LOW AND-frac (often wrong)
- Beams 1+: acoustically-grounded → lower log_prob (against LM prior), HIGH AND-frac (often correct)

This is the core mechanism enabling fairness-aware rescoring: at λ=1.0 the model promotes
the high-AND-frac hypothesis over the LM-biased one for accented speakers.
