## Hypothesis: Do Emotion-Sensitive Neurons Coincide with gc(k) Peaks for Emotion-Contrastive Audio?

### 1. Background: ESN Mechanism (Zhao et al. 2602.23136 — inferred from Gap #20 notes)

- Zhao et al. identify **Emotion-Sensitive Neurons (ESNs)** in Large Audio-Language Models (LALMs)
- ESNs modulate safety-relevant behavior: medium-intensity emotion → highest jailbreak risk (non-monotonic)
- Behavioral explanation: emotion overrides safety alignment, but the *mechanistic pathway* is unknown
- Key question: Are ESNs concentrated in the same layers as the model's audio **grounding peak**?

### 2. gc(k) Framework Recap (Paper A)

- **gc(k)** = grounding coefficient at layer k = how much model behavior depends on actual audio input vs. text priors
- Computed via activation patching: patch layer k activations from audio-contrastive pair → measure ΔP(correct token)
- **Listen Layer** = argmax_k gc(k) — the layer where audio grounding is highest
- Predicted gc(k) profile (from Lin et al. intermediate layer finding): peak at L_mid (~40-60% depth), not upper layers
- Upper layers (L_late) → text prediction priors dominate → gc(k) falls back toward 0

### 3. Core Hypothesis

**H1 (Coincidence):** ESNs are concentrated in layers where gc(k) is HIGH (near Listen Layer).
- Rationale: if emotion is grounded in the audio signal, the model processes it where audio grounding is strongest
- Prediction: ESN-layer distribution correlates positively with gc(k) profile across layers

**H2 (Dissociation):** ESNs are concentrated in L_late where gc(k) is LOW (text-dominated layers).
- Rationale: emotion may be re-constructed from semantic context (text prior) rather than acoustic signal
- Prediction: emotion "jailbreak" pathway = **modality collapse pathway** (model ignores audio, uses text priors)
- Implication: emotion-induced safety failure = a FAILURE of audio grounding, not a feature of audio grounding

**H3 (Bridge):** ESNs span BOTH L_mid (audio-grounded emotion extraction) AND L_late (safety neuron suppression).
- Two-stage mechanism: audio emotion decoded at L_mid → ESN-mediated suppression at L_late
- Prediction: patching at L_mid disrupts emotion detection; patching at L_late disrupts safety bypass

### 5. Falsification Criteria

| Hypothesis | Falsified if |
|---|---|
| H1 | ESN-layer distribution peaks AFTER gc(k) falls below 0.3 |
| H2 | ESN-layer distribution shows significant density at L_mid (where gc(k) > 0.5) |
| H3 | ESN distribution is unimodal (peaks only at L_mid OR only at L_late, not both) |

**Critical test**: Compute Pearson correlation(gc(k), ESN-density-per-layer). 
- H1: r > 0.7
- H2: r < -0.3 or r ≈ 0 (dissociated)
- H3: Non-linear relationship; bimodal ESN distribution regardless of gc(k) correlation
