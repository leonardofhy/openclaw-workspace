## Paper
**Discovering and Causally Validating Emotion-Sensitive Neurons in Large Audio-Language Models**
Zhao, Schuller, Sisman (JHU + Imperial) — arXiv:2601.03115, Jan 2026

## Problem
No mechanistic account of how LALMs encode emotion internally.
First neuron-level causal study in Qwen2.5-Omni, Kimi-Audio, Audio Flamingo 3.

## Method
1. Hook SwiGLU gate activations (g = SiLU(u)) from MLP blocks on correctly-solved SER items
2. Compute per-neuron, per-emotion stats: frequency P(e), magnitude M(e)
3. 4 selectors: LAP (frequency), LAPE (entropy), MAD (mean activation diff), contrast
4. Interventions: ablation (deactivate) + gain-based steering (amplify)

## Key Results
- **Self/cross structure**: ablating emotion-e ESNs → large drop on emotion e, small drop on others (causal validation)
- **Selector quality**: MAD + contrast >> LAP + entropy (magnitude matters more than frequency)
- **Layer clustering**: Non-uniform — ESNs cluster at early (0), early-mid (6-8), late (19-22) decoder layers
- **Transferability**: Partial cross-dataset transfer (category- and dataset-specific)
- **Non-additive joint amplification**: Amplifying union of all emotion ESN masks yields weaker steering than targeted — implies interaction/interference

## Connection to Q118 (AND/OR Gate × Emotion Neurons)

### Core hypothesis
Emotion ESNs in LALMs should preferentially be **AND-gates** (require both audio paralinguistics + text semantics) rather than OR-gates (fire on either alone), because emotion recognition requires multimodal conjunction.

### Evidence from paper supporting this
1. **Non-additive joint amplification** = AND-gate signature: if ESNs were simple OR-gates (fire on either modality), joint amplification would stack additively. Non-additivity suggests gated conjunction.
2. **MAD selector outperforms frequency** = selectivity requires both conditions to be present (conjunction selectivity vs. marginal frequency).
3. **Layer clustering at 19-22** = late multimodal fusion layers → late-layer ESNs more likely AND-gates (both modalities processed by then).
4. **Early layer (0) ESNs** = likely OR-gate (modality-specific, not yet fused).

### Testable prediction for mock experiment
- Define AND-gate ESN: activation P_combined >> max(P_audio, P_text)  (ratio > threshold θ)
- Define OR-gate ESN: P_combined ≈ max(P_audio, P_text)
- Prediction: AND-gate fraction peaks in layers 6-8 and 19-22; OR-gate fraction higher in layers 0-4
- ESNs selected by MAD/contrast should have higher AND-gate fraction than LAP/entropy-selected ESNs
