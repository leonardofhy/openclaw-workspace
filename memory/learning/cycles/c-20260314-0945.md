## Paper: ALMGuard (NeurIPS 2025)
**arXiv**: 2510.26096  
**Authors**: Jin, Cao, Su, Xue, Hao, Xu, Dong, Wang

### Problem
Audio jailbreaks bypass ALM safety. Text-LLM and audio-adversarial defenses don't transfer.

### Method
1. "Safety-aligned shortcuts naturally exist in ALMs" — key assumption
2. Find universal Shortcut Activation Perturbations (SAPs) that trigger these shortcuts at inference
3. Mel-Gradient Sparse Mask (M-GSM): restrict SAP to Mel bins sensitive to jailbreaks, not speech
4. Result: reduces jailbreak success to 4.6% across 4 ALMs
