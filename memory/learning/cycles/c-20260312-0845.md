## Problem
Are speech LLMs actually "listening" to audio, or doing implicit ASR internally?
Widely assumed speech LLMs > ASR→LLM cascades because they see raw audio — is this true?

## Method
1. **Matched-backbone testing**: isolates speech processing from LLM reasoning (pairs a speech LLM with its text-only backbone LLM)
2. **Logit lens + LEACE**: shows literal text transcript emerging progressively in hidden states
3. **Causal necessity test**: text representations causally necessary — patching them out degrades output

## Results
- Most deployed speech LLMs ARE expensive cascades (audio → implicit transcript → reasoning)
- Under noise: speech LLMs are WORSE cascades than explicit ASR→LLM (-7.6% at 0dB SNR)
- Clean-condition advantages reverse under real-world noise

## Connection to Research Tracks
