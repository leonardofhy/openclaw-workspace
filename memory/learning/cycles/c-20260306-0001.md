## §3.8 Content Summary (5 subsections)

1. **Primary metric**: gc(L) = DAS-IIA as the grounding coefficient; L* declared when CI at L*
   doesn't overlap CIs at L*±1 AND lower bound > chance + 0.05.
2. **Bootstrap CI protocol**: 1000 bootstrap resamples per layer; why NOT permutation test
   (breaks causal structure) and why NOT effect-size threshold (undefensible to reviewers).
3. **Baseline comparisons**: (a) random-init DAS (upper-bound sanity), (b) vanilla activation
   patching without rotation (vs DAS), (c) MFA unsupervised pre-screen (convergent validity),
   (d) trivial flat-gc baseline (chance).
4. **Evaluation conditions per experiment**: E1 (Whisper-small) vs E2 (Qwen2-Audio-7B) — what
   constitutes a passing result for each, what failure looks like, what alternative interpretation.
5. **Reporting standard**: report gc(L) per phoneme class (not just mean) to diagnose Risk A6;
   report full (probe_layer × intervene_layer) 2D heatmap, not just gc(L) diagonal.
