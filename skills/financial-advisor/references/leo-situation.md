## ⚖️ Legal Guardrails (僑生 Work Rules)

**MUST comply — penalties are severe.**

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **Work permit** | Required for ALL paid work. ezwp.wda.gov.tw, NT$100, ~7 days, via NTU 僑生輔導組 |
| **Semester cap** | ≤20 hr/week (includes RA + TA + tutoring + any other work) |
| **Break cap** | No weekly cap (subject to 勞基法: 8hr/day, 40hr/week) |
| **Grey area** | International freelancing (Upwork) — technically work in Taiwan, enforcement lax |
| **Malaysian bank acct** | NOT a loophole — jurisdiction based on where labor performed |
| **Penalties** | NT$30,000-150,000 fine, deportation + 3-year reentry ban, or lifetime work ban |
| **RA hours** | Already counts toward 20hr/week cap! Budget remaining hours carefully |

**Safest grey-area activities**: Digital product sales + passive income from already-created courses (no continuous labor).

### Tax (as resident ≥183 days/year)
- **5%** on first NT$590,000/year — most student income falls here
- Foreign-source income exempt up to NT$7.5M/year under 基本稅額條例
- Income from labor in Taiwan paid from abroad = likely Taiwan-source income
